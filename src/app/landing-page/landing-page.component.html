Our solution

We use a symmetric public and private key system built on the secure and efficient API recommended by the W3 Consortium. We only store the public keys and the user-encrypted private keys.

This means that even stealing our entire database will not grant access to any data: all data is only accessible by their owners. The important keys to data - user passwords - are never stored, they can only be seen by the people who own the data and companies who have collected the data.
